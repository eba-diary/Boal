
Feb. 16th 1915.

Following telegram sent Mrs. Andrews last night. Uncles condition not satisfactory appears to be failing. Future uncertain. Henry has written you about reading the letter and I will wire youagain tomorrow evening if any change. Today (16th)there has been no change for worse and I will keep 
you advised. Everything that affection and medical science can do is being done for him,

Theodore Davis Boal. 

Copy of telegram to Mrs. Newberry, N. Wilson & Dudley Dean.
